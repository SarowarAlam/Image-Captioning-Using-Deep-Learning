Image Captioning

The goal of picture captioning is to create a brief textual description of a given image. Despite the fact that it appears to be a simple activity for humans, computers find it challenging since it requires the ability to assess images (computer vision) and deliver a human-like description (natural language understanding). In the form of visual captioning, encoder-decoder designs have lately achieved advanced results.

Bangladesh Heritage

Bangladesh's cultural legacy is evidenced by numerous relics from ancient times, as well as ruins of prehistoric urban sites and other outstanding monuments from various eras scattered across the country. 448 sites have been designated as national heritage sites so far.

Combining Together

Bangladesh is a country with a lot of natural beauty. Rivers, beaches, ancient sites, sacred places, cliffs, woods, falls, tea gardens, and other natural features surround the area. The Sundarban, Bagerhat's Historic Mosque City, and the Ruins of the Buddhist Vihara at Paharpur are three of Bangladesh's 1007 earth heritage sites. The natural beauty of the areas attracts a large number of local and international tourists. Bangladesh, on the other hand, has struggled to develop itself as a tourism destination due to a number of obstacles. With that in mind, the purpose of this article is to test the usage of picture captioning to bring the natural beauty of Bangladesh to the attention of tourists. People all across the world will learn more about our country as a result of this endeavor.
